# Vegan Nutrition Plan for Endurance Cycling

## Daily Calorie & Macro Overview
- **Daily calories**: ~2,800-3,200 (depending on training intensity)
- **Protein**: 110-130g (1.5-1.8g per kg bodyweight)
- **Carbs**: 400-480g (5.5-6.5g per kg for endurance training)
- **Fats**: 70-90g (25% of calories)

## Core Protein Sources
- Legumes: lentils, chickpeas, beans
- Tofu & tempeh (fermented is easier to digest)
- Hemp seeds, pumpkin seeds
- Nutritional yeast
- Vegan protein powder (pea, hemp, or blended)
- Nuts and nut butters
- Whole grains (quinoa, buckwheat)

## Carb-Rich Cycling Fuel
- Oats, rice, pasta, potatoes, sweet potatoes
- Whole grain bread
- Bananas (great pre/post-ride)
- Dates and dried fruit (easy energy)

## Pre/During/Post Training
- **Pre-ride** (2-3h before): Oats + banana + almond butter
- **During** (long rides): Dates, energy bars, or sports drinks
- **Post-ride** (30-60min): Smoothie with protein powder + banana + berries

## Don't Forget
- Supplement **B12** (essential for vegans)
- Track iron intake (pair with vitamin C)
- Consider **omega-3s** (flax, chia, algae supplements)
